Medical supplies for XXX villages

$10,000 needed for 2014 - 2015

Provide much-needed medical supplies through indigenous NGO XXXAssist.

With an estimated population of only 1200, the XXX people of YYY are among the poorest and most threatened communities in the world. Based in the villages of ZZZ and ZZZ, they have seen their lands taken from them and they struggle to find help that is respectful of their culture and heritage.

In 2010, XXXAssist was formed to buy and distribute medical supplies to members of the community living in remote areas. These are places where simple first aid can save lives. XXXAssist distributes oral rehydration salts, antiseptic creams and bandages, and teaches hygenic practices for childbirth.

XXXAssist currently reaches 100 people in four villages through funds raised from the local community. They are seeking $10,000 to expand their work to a further 80 villages, and to hire a full-time nurse and community worker.

Freedom from persecution campaign

$5,000 needed for 2014

Help XXXLandRights to campaign for ownership of traditional landholdings.

With an estimated population of only 1200, the XXX people of YYY are among the poorest and most threatened communities in the world. Based in the villages of ZZZ and ZZZ, they have seen their lands taken from them and they struggle to find help that is respectful of their culture and heritage.

Despite a strong agrarian heritage, few XXX live on the land today. The Government of YYY has systematically removed their land and provided it to tennant farmers, who are managing the land badly. As a result crops have failed, and much of the land is unused.

XXXLandRights have been working since 2005 to have their traditional land holdings recognised under YYY law, but have had little progress. They want to raise $5,000 to mount this campaign, and are also looking for a volunteer with experience running similar international campaigns to work with them to promote their cause.
